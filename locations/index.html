By Car

Although the church has no car park there is on street parking nearby or, as it is within the town centre, there are supermarket car parks within a few minutes' walk.

Limited on-site car parking available at The County Hotel for 19 cars on a first-come-first-served basis at an additional charge.

By Taxi

Newcastle to Jarrow we would recommend Dean Taxis tel: 0191 444 4444. Jarrow to Newcastle, Dial-A-Cab Express Taxis has a taxi rank a few minutes walk from the church tel: 0191 455 8888.

By Metro

Jarrow metro station is a few minutes' walk from the Church.

By Bus

Go North East Crusader 27 bus runs between Newcastle city centre and Jarrow.

By Train

Newcastle Railway Station is opposite the hotel.

By Plane

Newcastle Airport is on the north west outskirts of Newcastle and about a half hour drive from Jarrow.
